welcome to the official website for Still Light. Still light is a computer game built in Game Maker Studio 2. It is a 2D, top-down, survival  horror  experience heavily inspired by groundbreaking genre classics Resident Evil and Dead Space. The game is full of characters trying to survive in the orbit of jupiter on the frozen world-moon Europa.
